Setting the scene

The Central Depository is responsible for managing the allocation of funds, equipment, and supplies to clubs and organizations. The Central Depository is also responsible for maintaining accurate records of all transactions and ensuring that all clubs and organizations are in compliance with the budget and regulations of the college. The Central Depository is an essential part of the college community, and it is important that it operates efficiently and effectively. Newly hired at the Central Depository, I noticed the following problems: Cluttered Emails with procurement requests from clubs, a lack of specific per-club accounting and well defined spending metrics, and a lack of a centralized database for club liabilities.
To address these issues, I developed a system that would streamline the procurement process, provide detailed accounting for each club, and create a centralized database for club liabilities.
